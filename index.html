Matrix Splitter (20×25)

How to Use:
  • Add cut lines: Left click on a row or column header to add a cut line.
  • Extract sub‑matrix: Left click and drag inside the grid to draw a rectangle; release to extract it as a smaller matrix below.
  • Snap to cuts: Hold Shift while dragging to snap edges to your cut lines.
  • Undo: Left-click sub-matrix. Delete/Backspace removes the currently hovered cut.
  • Remove cut lines: Left click on a row or column header cut line to remove cut line.
Built with D3.js. Direct manipulation inspired by Shneiderman’s principles.
